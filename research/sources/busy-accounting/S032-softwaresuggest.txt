With BUSY billing software, you can record and process business accounting transactions. It also offers modules such as reporting and analysis tools, user-configurable documents, trading excise registers, payroll, service tax, and TDS (tax deduction at source). What is BUSY Software? BUSY accounting software is a robust GST-compliant business accounting software that makes it easier for you to manage business finances, including return filing. With BUSY, you can generate GST-compliant invoices, E-Way bills, and E-Invoice IRN. It also has a GSTIN / HSN validation feature that helps you ensure valid GSTIN of the party and HSN of the items entered, resulting in error-free transactions. As part of my role as an IT consultant and sys admin for a local milling company (where I serve as a freelancer), I've strategically deployed Busy software on a Windows cloud server, providing essential support for our day-to-day activities. The software stands out for its excellence, receiving regular updates and seamlessly aligning with the intricacies of the Indian system, including GST and other tax compliances. The backup utility (Busy Agent) ensures safe backups on Google Cloud. Busy has become our go-to solution for billing, accounting, reporting, and various other functions, offering reliable performance without any hitches. The only challenge we've encountered is its dependency on the .NET stack, restricting our hosting options to Windows servers, which can be economically inefficient. A pivotal improvement would be a rewrite of the software using a Linux-friendly tech stack. This would empower us to explore more cost-effective hosting solutions, such as affordable VPS or dedicated Linux hosting, effectively curbing yearly costs. While Busy supports cloud hosting, our specific needs necessitate deployment only on IaaS. Shifting to a Linux-friendly tech stack would not only enhance flexibility but also yield substantial long-term cost benefits, aligning seamlessly with our company's efficiency goals.
