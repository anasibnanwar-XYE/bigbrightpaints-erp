Gain a brief understanding of its unique features and exceptional benefits. TallyPrime is as an all-in-one business management software that helps streamline operations, enhance efficiency, and boost productivity. It integrates multiple business functions into a single platform. Key Features: Comprehensive Management: It handles accounting, inventory, e-invoicing, banking, and cash/credit management. GST and Tax Compliance: The software is designed to make GST compliance easy, offering direct uploading of GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B, auto-GST reconciliation, e-invoice and e-way bill generation, and management of TDS and TCS. Connected Solutions: TallyPrime provides connected services, including a "WhatsApp for Business" integration, auto-bank reconciliation, and direct filing/uploads for GST. Remote Access: It allows users to access business reports online from any device, anywhere, and offers cloud access options. Insightful Reporting: The platform features a real-time, personalized reporting engine with a dashboard, various report views, and analytical options to help in decision-making. Plans and Pricing: TallyPrime Silver: This plan is for single-user access on a single PC. TallyPrime Gold: This plan is for multi-user access on multiple PCs. Both plans are available with monthly, quarterly, annual, or lifetime (perpetual) licenses and include all features. TallyPrime is simple to use, can be set up in minutes, and offers data safety and security.
